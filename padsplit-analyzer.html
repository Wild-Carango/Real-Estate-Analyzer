Section 03

The BRRRR Method

Buy · Rehab · Rent · Refinance · Repeat — how you scale from one deal to a portfolio.

B

Buy

Use SBL ($60K–$75K) to act like a cash buyer. Target distressed sellers. Offer $65K, settle at $70K max. Speed and certainty is your edge over financed buyers.

R

Rehab

$30K hard cap. Fix major systems first (HVAC, electrical, plumbing). Convert living/dining to rooms. Install smart thermostat locked at 72–76°F. Durable LVP flooring, no luxury finishes.

R

Rent (PadSplit)

Fill 6–7 rooms at $140–$155/week. Stabilize at 85–90% occupancy. This step is critical — lenders want to see income before they refi. Aim for 60 days of stable cash flow.

R

Refinance

At 75% LTV on a $150K ARV, your loan is $112,500. After paying back your $100K investment and ~$5K closing costs, you recover most or all of your capital to redeploy.

R

Repeat

Use returned capital for property #2. Same system. Each property adds $800–$1,200/month. Three properties = $2,400–$3,600/month without adding significant new capital.
